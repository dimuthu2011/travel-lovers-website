SRILANKA PLACES

🗺️ Top Travel Destinations in Sri Lanka

Sigiriya Rock Fortress

📍 Location: Dambulla, Central Province 📝 Details: Built in the 5th century AD by King Kashyapa. UNESCO World Heritage Site. Climb to the top for panoramic views of forests and reservoirs. Features ancient frescoes and the famous Mirror Wall.yy

🏛️ Temple of the Tooth (Sri Dalada Maligawa)

📍 Location: Kandy, Central Province

Houses a sacred relic: a tooth of Lord Buddha. UNESCO World Heritage Site. Situated near Kandy Lake, great for scenic walks. Major festival: Esala Perahera (July–August).

🛕 Dambulla Cave Temple

📍 Location: Central Province, Sri Lanka

🏛️ UNESCO World Heritage Site 🌄 Highlights: 5 cave temples, Buddha statues, ancient murals 🗓️ Best time to visit: All year round

🌄 Ella

📍 Location: Uva Province

Famous for breathtaking mountain views and tea plantations. Top attractions: ✔️ Nine Arches Bridge ✔️ Little Adam's Peak ✔️ Ella Rock Perfect for hiking and relaxing.
